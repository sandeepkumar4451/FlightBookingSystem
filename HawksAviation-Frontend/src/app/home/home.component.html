Steps to follow for Booking a Ticket

  1. Register Yourself
  2. Login by entering your credentials
  3. Go to Book Flight, enter your From and To stations and Date of Journey
  4. Choose a flight which suits for you and pay the fare amount
  5. You will receive a email notification once the payment is successful
  6. A Booking Id is generated through which you can checkin or cancel the flight
  7. Once checked in you will receive confirmation status, if cancelled the data will be removed
  8. If stuck somewhere reach our from contact us tab we are always ready to make your travel easy and fast
